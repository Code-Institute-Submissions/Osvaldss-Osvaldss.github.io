Biography

About Lilas and Innomine

Lilas and Innomine is a music duo from Vilnius, formed in 2009. The band has released 5 albums, winning Lithuanian hip-hop music, M.A.M.A. awards in 2017. The stage names Lilas and Innomine both originated at different times. According to K. Kiver-Lilo, he got his pseudonym in adolescence while performing with a friend. "My colleague was much taller than me.

Read more ->

We looked funny on stage, so we called him Bear, and I was called Lilas (little),” he says. The nickname of R. Venckys-Innomine was originally the abbreviation of his name - Rolis, but to him it didn't look professional: "I thought for a long time. I wanted abstraction, not a specific stage name, so that's how I got the pseudonym - Innomine" Lilas and Innomine - perform in the biggest arenas of Lithuania, and their works gather millions of views on YouTube. "But this is nothing special," says Konstantinas Kiveris (26) and Rolandas Venckys (27), who is known in Lithuania as the most popular hip-hop duo of today.
