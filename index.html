Biography

 

About Lilas and Innomine

Lilas and Innomine - perform in the biggest arenas of Lithuania, and their works gather millions of views on YouTube. "But this is nothing special," says Konstantinas Kiveris (26) and Rolandas Venckys (27), who is known in Lithuania as the most popular hip-hop duo of today. Lilas and Innomine is a music duo from Vilnius, formed in 2009. The band has released 5 albums, winning Lithuanian hip-hop music, M.A.M.A. awards in 2017.
